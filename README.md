# Yakkl Overview

Yakkl is a powerful, open source team chat application that combines the immediacy of real-time chat with the productivity benefits of threaded conversations. Yakkl can be used by open source projects, Fortune 500 companies, large standards bodies, and others who need a real-time chat system that allows users to easily process hundreds or thousands of messages a day.

## Getting started

Click on the appropriate link below. If nothing seems to apply, join us on the [Yakkl community server](https://yakkl.readthedocs.io/en/latest/contributing/chat-yakkl-org.html) and tell us what's up!

You might be interested in:

* **Contributing code**. Check out our [guide for new contributors](https://yakkl.readthedocs.io/en/latest/overview/contributing.html) to get started. Yakkl prides itself on maintaining a clean and well-tested codebase, and a stock of hundreds  [beginner-friendly issues][beginner-friendly].

* **Contributing non-code**.
  [Report an issue](https://yakkl.readthedocs.io/en/latest/overview/contributing.html#reporting-issue), [translate](https://yakkl.readthedocs.io/en/latest/translating/translating.html) Yakkl into your language, [write](https://yakkl.readthedocs.io/en/latest/overview/contributing.html#yakkl-outreach) for the Yakkl blog, or [give us feedback](https://yakkl.readthedocs.io/en/latest/overview/contributing.html#user-feedback). We would love to hear from you, even if you're just trying the product out.

* **Supporting Yakkl**. Advocate for your organization to use Yakkl, write a review in the mobile app stores, or [upvote Yakkl](https://yakkl.readthedocs.io/en/latest/overview/contributing.html#yakkl-outreach) on product comparison sites.

* **Checking Yakkl out**. The best way to see Yakkl in action is to drop by the:
  [Yakkl community server](https://yakkl.readthedocs.io/en/latest/contributing/chat-yakkl-org.html). We also recommend reading Yakkl for [open source](https://yakkl.com/for/open-source/), Yakkl for [companies](https://yakkl.com/for/companies/), or Yakkl for  [working groups and part time communities](https://yakkl.com/for/working-groups-and-communities/).

* **Running a Yakkl server**. Setting up a server takes   just a couple of minutes. Yakkl runs on Ubuntu 18.04 Bionic, Ubuntu 16.04 Xenial. The installation process is [documented here](https://yakkl.readthedocs.io/en/stable/production/install.html).
  Commercial support is available; see <https://yakkl.com/plans> for details.

* **Using Yakkl without setting up a server**. <https://yakkl.com> offers free and commercial hosting.

You may also be interested in reading our [blog](https://blog.yakkl.com/) or following us on [twitter](https://twitter.com/yakklofficial).
