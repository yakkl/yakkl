If you use our “Retail” or “Consumer” products/services, we may process Personal Information such as:

Base level (free or paid)

  • Name; Username; Email; Login preference (e.g., Google, Apple, X, or others);
  • (Paid only) payment method, billing details, and billing history.

Advanced security level (paid)

  • Pincode (transaction verification); Emergency kit (recovery);
