1. Subscription Tiers

YAKKL offers multiple subscription tiers to meet different user needs:

Free Tier: Basic wallet functionality including standard transaction features, limited blockchain support, and essential security features. Subject to usage limitations and may include reduced priority during high-demand periods.

Pro-level Tiers: Enhanced features including but not limited to: additional blockchain support, advanced security features, priority support, lower swap fees, higher transaction limits, portfolio analytics, and API access. Specific features vary by tier level.

Enterprise Tier: (Coming Soon) Custom solutions for businesses and institutions with dedicated support, SLAs, and compliance features. Contact sales for details.

Current tier features and pricing are displayed at yakkl.com/pricing and within the application. Features may change with reasonable notice.

2. Billing Cycles

Subscriptions are available in the following billing cycles:

  • Monthly: Billed every calendar month from subscription start date
  • Annual: Billed yearly from subscription start date (includes discount)
  • Custom: Enterprise customers may negotiate custom billing terms

Your billing cycle begins on the date you first subscribe and renews on the same calendar day each period. If the renewal date doesn't exist in a given month (e.g., 31st), billing occurs on the last day of that month.

3. Payment Methods

Cryptocurrency Payments (Preferred):

  • Accepted on most major networks (Ethereum, Polygon, Arbitrum, Optimism, Base, etc.)
  • Native tokens preferred; ERC-20/compatible tokens accepted where supported
  • Payment amount calculated at time of transaction initiation
  • You are responsible for all network fees, gas costs, and any swap fees
  • Transactions must complete within 1 hour of initiation or may require retry
  • Insufficient wallet balance will result in failed renewal; service continues for 3-day grace period
  • We'll send reminder emails 3 days before crypto subscription renewals

Credit Card Payments:

  • Available for select services only
  • Card details stored securely by our payment processor (not YAKKL)
  • Automatic retry on failed payments for up to 7 days
  • International cards accepted; billed in USD

Prepaid Credits:

  • Purchase credits in advance for future subscription payments
  • Credits expire 12 months from purchase date
  • Non-refundable and non-transferable
  • Applied automatically before charging payment method

4. Automatic Renewal

All paid subscriptions automatically renew to ensure uninterrupted service. By subscribing, you authorize recurring charges to your selected payment method.

Renewal Process:

  • Renewal attempted up to 24 hours before subscription expiry
  • Email notification sent 3-7 days before renewal (check spam filters)
  • For crypto: ensure sufficient balance including gas fees
  • For cards: ensure card is valid and has sufficient funds

To disable automatic renewal, cancel your subscription at least 24 hours before the renewal date. Cancelled subscriptions remain active until period end.

5. Upgrades and Downgrades

Upgrades:

  • Take effect immediately upon payment
  • Prorated charge for remainder of current billing period
  • Future renewals at new tier price
  • Instant access to upgraded features

Downgrades:

  • Take effect at start of next billing period
  • No partial refunds for current period
  • Maintain current tier features until downgrade effective
  • Data/features may be limited after downgrade; export important data beforehand

Switching Billing Cycles: Moving from monthly to annual billing provides immediate discount benefits. Moving from annual to monthly takes effect at next renewal without refund of annual discount.

6. Cancellation and Refunds

Cancellation:

  • Cancel anytime through account settings or by contacting support

  • Re-subscribe anytime (may lose promotional pricing)
  • Data retained per our Privacy Policy

Refund Policy:

  • No refunds except where required by law
  • No refunds for partial periods, downgrades, or unused credits
  • No refunds for crypto payment gas/network fees
  • Disputed charges subject to Terms of Service arbitration clause

We may offer free trials for pro-level features:

  • Trial length varies by promotion (typically 14 days)

Trial terms displayed during signup. Abuse of trials may result in immediate termination and charges.

Each tier includes specific usage allowances:

Exceeding limits may result in:

  • Temporary service restrictions

Current limits displayed in account dashboard. We may adjust limits with 30 days notice.

9. Price Changes

We may change subscription prices with advance notice:

  • 30 days notice for price increases via email

  • Grandfathered pricing may be offered to existing subscribers
  • Annual subscribers protected from increases until renewal

If you disagree with price changes, cancel before they take effect. Continued use constitutes acceptance of new pricing.

10. Failed Payments

Grace Period: 3-day grace period for failed renewals during which service continues. Multiple payment retry attempts during grace period.

After Grace Period:

  • Subscription suspended (not cancelled)
  • Limited to free tier features
  • Premium features disabled but data preserved
  • Reactivate by updating payment method
  • Extended suspension (30+ days) may result in data deletion

Crypto-Specific Failures:

  • Insufficient gas: transaction won't initiate
  • Insufficient token balance: payment fails
  • Network congestion: retry with higher gas
  • Smart contract issues: contact support
