YAKKL® Generative AI User Guidelines

Last Updated: July 30, 2025

These Guidelines govern your use of generative AI, agents, MCP servers, and related features (collectively “Generative AI Features”) provided by YAKKL, Inc. (“YAKKL”®). If your agreement with YAKKL incorporates our General Terms of Use, then your use of Generative AI Features is also governed by the YAKKL Product Specific Terms, which are incorporated herein by reference.

1. No AI/ML Model Training

You may only use YAKKL Generative AI Features for legitimate creative, productivity, or business purposes associated with our platform. Under no circumstances may you use (or permit others to use) any content, data, output, or information received from these features to create, train, test, or otherwise improve artificial intelligence, machine learning models, or related algorithms.

2. Respectful and Safe Use

Do not utilize YAKKL Generative AI Features in violation of the law, regulations, or in a manner harmful to others. Prohibited uses include but are not limited to:

  • Creating or disseminating pornographic material or explicit nudity
  • Creating hateful or highly offensive content targeting any group based on race, ethnicity, national origin, religion, disability, gender, age, or sexual orientation
  • Glorifying violence, gore, or self-harm
  • Exploiting or depicting minors in any sexual manner
  • Promoting terrorism or violent extremism
  • Publishing misleading, fraudulent, or deceptive content that could result in real-world harm
  • Violating privacy or data protection rights of others
  • Engaging in regulated activities without proper compliance

Prompts and outputs from gen AI services may be reviewed, automatically or manually, for abuse prevention and content safety.

3. Authenticity and Integrity

YAKKL disables or suspends accounts engaged in deceptive or harmful conduct, including but not limited to:

  • Using false, misleading, or inaccurate information in user profiles or outputs
  • Impersonating individuals or entities
  • Using unauthorized automation (such as bulk or scripted uploads)
  • Manipulating engagement statistics fraudulently

4. Respect for Intellectual Property

You are prohibited from using YAKKL Generative AI Features to create, upload, or share content in violation of third-party copyright, trademark, privacy, publicity, or other legal rights. This includes bypassing controls designed to prevent such use. If you believe your rights have been violated by a YAKKL user, contact legal@yakkl.com.

5. Use Judgment and Validate Outputs

Generated outputs may occasionally be inaccurate or misleading. You are responsible for reviewing and validating all results.

6. No Professional Advice

YAKKL’s Generative AI Features are not a substitute for legal, financial, medical, or other professional advice, and no client/professional relationship is formed by using these features.

7. Transparency and Content Credentials

In order to enhance transparency, YAKKL may attach credentials or watermarks to content generated by our AI features, indicating its origin.

8. Commercial Use

Unless otherwise expressly designated, outputs from YAKKL Generative AI Features may be used in commercial projects. If a feature is in beta and not permitted for commercial use, its outputs are for personal use only.

9. Non-AI Content and Additional Terms

Some outputs may contain or reference non-generative content. Such content is subject to your agreement with YAKKL.

10. Enforcement and Contact

YAKKL may act against accounts or content violating these Guidelines. For more information, see the YAKKL Terms of Use. Contact: legal@yakkl.com
