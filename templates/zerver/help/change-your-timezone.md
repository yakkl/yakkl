# Change your timezone

Yakkl always uses your computer's timezone for displaying dates and
timestamps (note that you need to reload Yakkl after changing your
computer's timezone for changes to take effect).

By default, Yakkl also indirectly displays your timezone to others, via the
**Local time** field of your [user profile](/help/view-someones-profile).
You can change the timezone Yakkl displays to others.

### Change your timezone

{start_tabs}

{settings_tab|display-settings}
