# Set your profile picture

By default, Yakkl uses an image generated by
[Gravatar](https://en.gravatar.com/) as your profile picture.
If your email address already has a gravatar associated with it, Yakkl will
use your existing gravatar.

You can also upload a custom profile picture to Yakkl.

### Set your profile picture

{start_tabs}

{settings_tab|your-account}

2. Under **Profile picture**, click **Upload new profile picture** and choose an image to upload.

{end_tabs}
